About

I graduated in a Bachelor of Science: Game Art at the University of the Arts Utrecht. I love to make beautiful designs and concepts with a focus to translate the essence or story of my subject. I specialise in concept art and its ideation process, creating digital assets, illustrations and 3D work. This may seem like a little bit much, but I am just passionate about anything creative and because I have a big repertoire of technical and creative skills, but also a solid creative design process, I am able to convert this to many different specialisations. Besides illustrating, designing and the occasional coding, I love to be in nature which is one of my biggest inspirations. I am currently located in Auckland, New Zealand.
