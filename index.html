About

I graduated in a Bachelor of Science: Game Art at the Academy of the Arts Utrecht. I love to make beautiful designs and to translate these into visuals. My focus is natural interactions and a pleasant user experience but keeping well thought-out design and function. Besides illustrating, designing and the occasional coding, I love to be in nature which is one of my biggest inspirations. I am currently located in Auckland, New Zealand.
